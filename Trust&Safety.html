How does PhonePe keep
my payments safe?

We assign a risk score to every
transaction on the app. Transactions with
high risk scores are automatically blocked,
keeping your payment safe.

What can I do to keep my
transactions safe?

  • Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, CVV and
    credit/debit card details with anyone
  • Never enter your UPI PIN to receive
    money on PhonePe
  • Never download third party apps, such as
    AnyDesk and TeamViewer at the request
    of unknown callers
